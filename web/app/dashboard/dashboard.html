Welcome to Tradamus, a suite of tools for the creation and publication of scholarly editions. The power of digital data and the new possibilities in editorial assertion and annotation have been placed behind a helpful interface to allow for deep and accurate standards-based encoding of manuscripts, editorial assertions, collation, annotation and more without requiring the editor to learn another new language.

To begin, click the + Create link in your "Projects". If you get lost at any point, just look for the buttons.
